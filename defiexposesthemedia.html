DEFI Exposes the media & the FED.

Explaining how the US Economy works in DEFI terms & why the media & fed is wrong.

Staking = Buying bonds with $USD

More people staking = lower yields (also called interest rates by some)

People stake because they trust the project (government)

When people do not trust the project (government) they unstable their $USD (leading to increased amount of circulating $USD, also called inflation)

More people staking = less inflation because all the $USD is locked up

In simple terms:

  • When people stake (buying bonds) they lock up their $USD which decreases money supply & yield rates (APY).
  • When people unstake (selling bonds) they unlock their $USD which increases money supply & yield rates (APY).
